# Claim Troubleshooting Runbook

## Step-by-Step Troubleshooting

### Step 1: Check Eligibility
- Verify patient coverage
- Confirm service date is within coverage period

---

### Step 2: Validate Subscriber Information
- Check subscriber ID
- Confirm correct formatting

---

### Step 3: Verify Provider Details
- Ensure valid NPI
- Confirm provider enrollment

---

### Step 4: Check for Duplicate Claims
- Search claim history
- Avoid resubmitting existing claims

---

### Step 5: Validate Codes
- Diagnosis codes (ICD)
- Procedure codes (CPT/HCPCS)

---

### Step 6: Check Authorization
- Verify prior authorization requirement
- Confirm approval number

---

### Step 7: Validate Service Line Data
- Charge amount present
- Units and service details correct

---

## General Rule

Always validate:
- Member data
- Provider data
- Service data

before resubmitting a claim.
