# EDI 837 Overview

EDI 837 is a healthcare claim transaction used to submit billing information.

## Key Terms

Subscriber: The insured individual  
Provider: The entity submitting the claim  
Service Line: Individual billed service  
Eligibility: Whether coverage is active  

## Common Errors

Eligibility failure:
Coverage not active

Invalid subscriber ID:
ID missing or incorrect

Duplicate claim:
Same claim submitted multiple times
